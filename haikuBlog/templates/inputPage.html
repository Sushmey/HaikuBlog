Haiku is a japanese form of poetry, 17 syllabled poem
in a 5-7-5 pattern

this is the middle of it
end of this haiku

So the first line "This is a haiku" has 5 syllables,
next one has 7 syllables and last line has 5 syllables
thus making a haiku
